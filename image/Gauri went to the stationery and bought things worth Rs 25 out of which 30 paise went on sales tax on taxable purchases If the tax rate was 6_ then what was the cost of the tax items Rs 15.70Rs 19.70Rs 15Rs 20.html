Question

Gauri went to the stationery and bought things worth Rs 25 out of which 30 paise went on sales tax on taxable purchases If the tax rate was 6% then what was the cost of the tax free items?
  1. Rs 15.70
  2. Rs 19.70
  3. Rs 15
  4. Rs 20

A
Rs 15.70
B
Rs 19.70
C
Rs 20
D
Rs 15
Open in App
Solution
Verified by Toppr

Let the amount of taxable purchases be Rs.x

Given, 6% of x=30 paisa=30100Rs

⇒x=(30100×1006)=5

∴ Cost of tax free items =Rs.[25−(5+0.30)]=Rs.19.70
